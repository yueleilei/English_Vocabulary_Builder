### 10 Phrases for Disagreeing

I don't think so.  
I beg to differ.  
I'am afraid I don't agree.  
I'am not so sure about that.  
That's not how I see it.  
Not necessarily.  
Yes, but... (say your opinion)  
On the contrary.  
(very informal) No way!  
(very strong) I totally disagree.
